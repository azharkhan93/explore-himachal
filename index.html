FAQ

What should I pack for my trip?

A. Pack warm clothing (especially in winter), comfortable shoes for trekking, personal medications, and essentials like sunscreen and sunglasses.

How can I reach Himachal from Delhi?

1. By Air: You can take a flight to Shimla, Kullu (for Manali), and Kangra (for Dharamsala) from various cities across India. Once at the airport, you can easily find pre-paid taxis outside the terminals to reach your destination.

 

2. By Road: Himachal has well-connected roads, including 8 national highways and 19 state highways, offering excellent connectivity with major cities in India. You can either drive yourself or rent a cab from Delhi. There are also state-owned and private luxury bus services available to most large cities in Himachal.

 

3. By Train: Himachal Pradesh has a narrow-gauge track linking Pathankot in Punjab to Kangra and Kalka in Haryana to Shimla Himachal Pradesh. You can get on the Kalka-Shimla Toy Train for a six-hour journey through tunnels and breathtaking nature. Other trains on the Kalka-Shimla route include the Shivalik Deluxe Express, Rail Motor Car, Shimla Express, Shimla Kalka Passenger, and Himalayan Queen

Which are some of the must visit places in Himachal?

Manali Himachal Pradesh is one of the most visited places in the state. Its stunning natural beauty, including snow-capped peaks, lush forests, and picturesque valleys, attracts tourists from all over the world. The town also offers various adventure activities like trekking, skiing, and paragliding, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

What all adventure activities can be done in Himachal?

Some popular adventure activities in Himachal include trekking, river rafting, paragliding, skiing, snowboarding, camping, rock climbing, mountain biking, zip-lining, and jeep safari. You can explore the rugged terrain of the Himalayas or navigate the rapids of its rivers for an exhilarating experience

Where can we go for paragliding in Himachal Pradesh?

Here are some best paragliding spots in Himachal Pradesh:
Bir-Billing: Known as India’s paragliding hub, it offers great views and perfect winds for a thrilling ride.
Solang Valley, Manali: Famous for its stunning scenery, it’s a popular spot for paragliding, especially in summer.
Kangra Valley: With beautiful landscapes and good wind conditions, it’s a fantastic place to soar in the sky.

Which are some famous honeymoon destinations in Himachal Pradesh?

Himachal Pradesh offers several honeymoon destinations including Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala, Dalhousie, and Kasauli. These destinations offer romantic settings with picturesque landscapes, cozy accommodations, and opportunities for adventure and relaxation. You can enjoy exploring its snow-capped mountains, strolling through lush valleys, or getting serene lakeside views, with your better half to create unforgettable memories.

Is it safe to travel to Himachal Pradesh?

Yes, Himachal is generally safe for tourists. However, always stay updated on weather conditions and travel advisories.

When is the best time to visit Himachal Pradesh?

The best time to visit Himachal Pradesh is from February to June, which covers the spring and summer seasons. During this time, the temperatures range between 22°C to 37°C with warm days and cool nights. It is also ideal for outdoor adventures and less-crowded trekking trails. What is the local cuisine like?

Himachali cuisine features dishes like Chana Madra, Dham, Siddu, and various lentil preparations. Don’t miss out on local sweets like Sidu and Kullu Trout.

Can I visit Himachal Pradesh with children?

Yes, Himachal is family-friendly with various activities suitable for children, including sightseeing, nature walks, and adventure sports.
